Our Story

Built for tiny hands,
loved by tired parents

TotTaps is a free, safe keyboard toy that turns your baby's natural curiosity into a joyful, chaotic, colourful experience — with zero risk to your files.

How it started

Every parent knows this moment

You're working from home, on an important call, trying to hit a deadline. Your toddler crawls over, sees the glowing keyboard, and those tiny hands start reaching for it.

You panic. Emails get sent. Files get deleted. Browser tabs multiply. The keyboard is irresistible to little ones — and terrifying for parents.

TotTaps was built to solve exactly that moment. Open it, tap Start, go fullscreen — and let your baby tap away safely while you breathe again. Every keypress turns into letters, numbers, colours, sounds and shooting stars. No damage. Pure delight.

It's completely free, needs no account, and works on any device your toddler can get their hands on — laptop, tablet, phone or desktop.

Features

What TotTaps does

Every feature was designed around one question: will a 1-year-old love it and will a parent trust it?

⌨️
Letters & Numbers Speak
Press A and the app says "A" out loud. Press 3 and it says "three". Every key teaches while it entertains.
🔒
Fullscreen Kiosk Mode
Goes fully fullscreen with accidental-exit protection. Works with iPad Guided Access and Android screen pinning.
🎨
6 Beautiful Themes
Party, Jungle, Ocean, Space, Dino and Diwali — each with its own colours, sounds and particle effects.
⭐
Stars & Shooting Stars
A living starfield glows in the background. Tap more and shooting stars streak across the screen more often.
🔑
Special Key Illustrations
Shift shows a hot air balloon. Ctrl launches a racing car. Backspace triggers a volcano. Every key is a surprise.
👪
Hidden Parent Panel
Hold the top-left corner for 2 seconds to open controls. Set timers, change themes, toggle sounds — invisible to toddlers.
⏱️
Session Timer
Set a 2, 5, 10 or 15-minute screen time limit. The session ends with a fun Smash Report card your toddler can be proud of.
🌪️
Chaos Progression
The screen evolves as your baby taps. Calm → Warming Up → Wild → Chaos Mode → Legendary. More taps = bigger explosions.

Privacy & safety

Your family's data stays your family's

TotTaps was designed with children's safety as the first priority, not an afterthought.

🚫

No accounts required

Open and play instantly. No email, no login, no profile ever created.

👤

No personal data collected

No names, ages, locations, or any identifying information is ever stored.

📊

Anonymous analytics only

Only aggregate counts (like total daily taps) are measured — never individual sessions.

🚫

Zero ads, ever

No advertising, no sponsors, no promoted content. The experience is 100% for the child.

🔒

No third-party trackers

No Facebook pixel, no Google ads, no retargeting. Nothing that follows your family around the web.

🌐

Works offline after first load

Once loaded, TotTaps works without an internet connection — great for travel.
